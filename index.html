HELLO

My name is Michael Grzesiek. I am a full time Twitch streamer and former professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) player.

MY SKILLS

Streaming

Since transitioning from a competitive CS:GO player to a full-time streamer on the platform Twitch, I have maintained a diverse set of games. I have streamed for over 6,000 hours on the platform, and have acquired over 330 million total views. I have played games like CS:GO, PUBG, Fortnite, Call of Duty: Black Ops 4, Battlefield V, Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege, Ring of Elysium, Apex Legends, Overwatch,etc...

Before Streaming

I started my CS:GO career with several ESEA teams, particularly Exertus and Manajuma. I was soon acquired by CompLexity Gaming as a stand-in, and signed with Cloud9 in August 2014 when CompLexity was acquired by Cloud9. I helped lead Cloud9 to 2nd place in ESL One Cologne 2017, and a first place finish at ESL Pro League Season 4 in 2016.
